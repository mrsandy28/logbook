Discover Luing

Luing is a small village nestled in the beautiful state of Sikkim, India.
It's located in the northeastern part of the country, surrounded by the lush greenery of the Himalayas.
The village offers stunning panoramic views, pristine natural beauty, and a serene atmosphere.
Luing is known for its vibrant culture, friendly locals, and traditional Sikkimese lifestyle.
It's a perfect destination for nature lovers, trekkers, and those looking to experience the tranquility of a remote village.
The area is also rich in biodiversity, featuring unique flora and fauna native to the region.
Whether you're visiting for a short trip or an extended stay,
Luing provides a peaceful retreat from the hustle and bustle of city life.

Brief Information

Fox Land

Fox Land is a unique and enchanting destination in Sikkim,
known for its lush greenery and serene environment.
It's a perfect spot for nature lovers and those looking to escape the hustle and bustle of city life.
Visitors can enjoy activities like trekking, bird watching, and simply relaxing amidst nature.

Banjhakri Falls and Energy Park

Banjhakri Falls is a beautiful waterfall located near Gangtok, Sikkim.
The falls are approximately 30 meters high and are surrounded by lush forests.
The Banjhakri Energy Park, developed around the falls, celebrates the traditional shamanic beliefs of the Sikkimese people.

Khelground

Khelground, located in Gangtok, is a popular sports ground where locals and visitors can engage in various outdoor activities.
It's a hub for sports enthusiasts and offers facilities for games like football, cricket, and other sports.
Khelground is a great place to enjoy some physical activity and socialize with locals.

These destinations offer a glimpse into the natural beauty and cultural richness of Sikkim.
